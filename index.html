fitness exercises

Fitness exercises are one of the most important exercises, as they include many exercises that help strengthen the body, in addition to giving it energy and vitality, as well as helping to reduce any muscle contractions, including gymnastics, lunges and cobra exercises.

dumbbell exercises

Dumbbells are one of the best machines that intensify the exercises just like the traditional drip sets, and they are better than the exercises with natural barbells. As dumbbell exercises are one of the most exercises that allow a large range of motion for the arm, more than the movement that can be achieved with regular push-ups or squats, expanding the range of motion increases the load and pressure on the muscle.

Bar exercises

Building the muscles of the body depends mainly on weight-lifting exercises, and other strength exercises that can be practiced at home or in the gym, but it is preferable to practice them in the gym under the supervision of a professional sports coach, in order to obtain the quality of exercises appropriate to the shape and weight of the body, in addition to The possibility of benefiting from all the sports equipment used in this field, and it is also recommended to consult a dietician to obtain an appropriate diet to build and strengthen the body.
